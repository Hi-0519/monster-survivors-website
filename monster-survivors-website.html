About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping action survival game where you must outlast increasingly difficult waves of monsters. As time progresses, your character grows stronger, unlocking new abilities and weapons to face the escalating challenge.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Attacks happen automatically as monsters come within range. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible to achieve the highest score!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from a roster of unique heroes, each with their own special abilities and starting weapons.

Weapon Arsenal

Collect and upgrade dozens of weapons and passive items to create your own unstoppable build.

Endless Challenges

Face procedurally generated levels for unlimited replayability and unique experiences.

Epic Boss Battles

Test your skills against massive bosses that appear every few minutes with increasing difficulty.

Survival Tips

Early Game Strategy

Focus on movement speed upgrades early to help you avoid monsters while your weapons are still weak. Prioritize experience gems to level up faster.

Mid Game Power

By the 10-minute mark, you should have a main weapon and 2-3 support weapons. Look for synergies between your items and character abilities.

Late Game Survival

After 20 minutes, enemies become extremely numerous. Area-of-effect weapons and defensive items become essential for survival.

Boss Strategy

When facing bosses, focus on constant movement in a circular pattern. This helps avoid their attacks while your weapons automatically damage them.
